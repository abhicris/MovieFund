# CEO & Product Roadmap - MovieFund Launch

## Executive Summary

This document outlines all non-technical work required to launch MovieFund's pilot program within 3-6 months. This includes legal, compliance, business development, investor relations, and operational setup.

**Target Launch**: 3-6 months from start date
**Pilot Phase**: 3-5 movies, 50-100 investors
**Goal**: Validate business model and prepare for seed round

---

## Timeline Overview

### Month 1: Foundation & Legal Setup
- Week 1-2: Legal structure and compliance
- Week 3-4: Business model finalization and pricing

### Month 2: Pilot Movie Selection & Partnerships
- Week 1-2: Identify and negotiate with 3-5 pilot movies
- Week 3-4: Partnership agreements and documentation

### Month 3: Investor Relations & Seed Round Prep
- Week 1-2: Seed round preparation
- Week 3-4: Investor meetings and pitch deck

### Month 4-6: Pilot Launch & Operations
- Month 4: Final preparations and soft launch
- Month 5: Pilot execution and monitoring
- Month 6: Results analysis and seed round closing

## 1. Legal & Compliance (Priority: Critical)

### 1.1 Entity Structure
- [ ] **Company Registration**
  - Register MovieFund as legal entity (Private Limited Company)
  - Choose jurisdiction (India/US/Singapore based on target market)
  - Register trademark for "MovieFund" / "MovieBitFund"
  - Timeline: 2-3 weeks

- [ ] **Regulatory Compliance**
  - Determine regulatory requirements (SEBI, SEC, or local equivalent)
  - Consult with securities lawyer
  - Understand fractional investment regulations
  - Timeline: 3-4 weeks

### 1.2 Legal Documentation
- [ ] **Terms of Service**
  - User terms and conditions
  - Investment terms and conditions
  - Risk disclosures
  - Timeline: 2 weeks

- [ ] **Privacy Policy**
  - GDPR compliance (if applicable)
  - Data protection policy
  - User data handling
  - Timeline: 1 week

- [ ] **Investment Agreements**
  - Movie investment contract template
  - Investor agreement template
  - Revenue sharing agreement
  - Timeline: 3-4 weeks

- [ ] **Partnership Agreements**
  - Production company partnership agreement
  - Revenue distribution agreement
  - Timeline: 2-3 weeks

### 1.3 Compliance & Licensing
- [ ] **Financial Services License**
  - Determine if investment platform license required
  - Apply for necessary licenses
  - Timeline: 4-8 weeks (varies by jurisdiction)

- [ ] **KYC/AML Compliance**
  - Set up KYC verification process
  - AML compliance framework
  - Partner with KYC provider (e.g., Jumio, Onfido)
  - Timeline: 2-3 weeks

- [ ] **Tax Compliance**
  - Tax structure for returns distribution
  - Investor tax documentation
  - Corporate tax planning
  - Timeline: 2-3 weeks

**Total Legal Timeline: 6-8 weeks**

## 2. Business Model & Pricing

### 2.1 Fee Structure
- [ ] **Platform Fees**
  - Structuring fee: 3-7% of capital raised per movie
  - Management fee: 1-2% AUM annually
  - Transaction fee: 0.5-1% per investment
  - Performance fee: 10-20% of returns above hurdle rate (optional)
  - Timeline: 1 week

- [ ] **Revenue Projections**
  - Pilot phase revenue model
  - Break-even analysis
  - 12-month financial projections
  - Timeline: 1 week

### 2.2 Pricing Strategy
- [ ] **Lot Pricing Model**
  - Confirm 0.1% of budget per lot
  - Minimum investment thresholds
  - Maximum investment limits per investor
  - Timeline: 1 week

- [ ] **Competitive Analysis**
  - Research competitor pricing
  - Market positioning
  - Value proposition refinement
  - Timeline: 1 week

**Total Pricing Timeline: 2-3 weeks**
