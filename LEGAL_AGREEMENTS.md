# Legal Agreements & Templates

## Overview

This document outlines all legal agreements required for MovieFund operations, including templates, checklists, and key terms.

---

## Required Agreements

### 1. Investment Agreement (Movie-Investor)

#### Purpose
Defines the terms of investment between individual investors and the movie project.

#### Key Terms
- Investment amount and number of lots
- Ownership percentage (0.1% per lot)
- Revenue sharing percentage
- Payment terms
- Rights and obligations
- Risk disclosures
- Dispute resolution

#### Template Sections
1. **Parties**
   - Investor details
   - Movie project details
   - MovieFund as intermediary

2. **Investment Terms**
   - Investment amount
   - Number of lots purchased
   - Price per lot
   - Total investment

3. **Ownership & Returns**
   - Ownership percentage
   - Revenue sharing formula
   - Distribution schedule
   - Tax implications

4. **Rights & Obligations**
   - Investor rights (information, returns)
   - Investor obligations (payment, no interference)
   - MovieFund obligations (transparency, distribution)

5. **Risk Disclosures**
   - Investment risks
   - No guarantee of returns
   - Possible loss of capital
   - Market risks

6. **Termination & Exit**
   - No early exit (unless secondary market)
   - Returns upon movie completion
   - Force majeure

7. **Dispute Resolution**
   - Governing law
   - Arbitration clause
   - Jurisdiction

**Legal Review Required**: Yes
**Execution**: Digital signature via platform

## Key Legal Considerations

### 1. Securities Regulations
- **Consideration**: Determine if fractional movie investments are considered securities
- **Action**: Consult securities lawyer
- **Compliance**: Register or qualify for exemption

### 2. Investment Limits
- **Consideration**: Maximum investment per investor
- **Action**: Set limits based on regulations
- **Compliance**: Enforce limits in platform

### 3. Risk Disclosures
- **Consideration**: Comprehensive risk disclosure required
- **Action**: Include all material risks
- **Compliance**: Clear, prominent disclosure

### 4. Tax Implications
- **Consideration**: Tax treatment of returns
- **Action**: Consult tax advisor
- **Compliance**: Provide tax documentation

### 5. Intellectual Property
- **Consideration**: Rights to use movie materials
- **Action**: Obtain necessary licenses
- **Compliance**: Respect IP rights
