# Movie Operations Guide

## Overview

This document outlines the operational processes for onboarding, managing, and monitoring movies on the MovieFund platform.

---

## Movie Onboarding Process

### Stage 1: Initial Contact & Qualification

#### 1.1 Lead Generation
- **Sources**:
  - Direct outreach to production companies
  - Film festivals and industry events
  - Referrals from existing partners
  - Industry databases and directories
  - Social media and online presence

#### 1.2 Initial Qualification Checklist
- [ ] Production company is registered and legitimate
- [ ] Movie is in pre-production or early production stage
- [ ] Budget range: $2M - $50M
- [ ] Director has previous credits
- [ ] Production timeline is realistic
- [ ] Commercial viability assessment positive

#### 1.3 Initial Meeting
- **Agenda**:
  - Introduction to MovieFund platform
  - Movie details and budget discussion
  - Funding requirements
  - Timeline and milestones
  - Revenue sharing model
  - Next steps

**Timeline**: 1-2 weeks

---

## Revenue Tracking & Distribution

### Revenue Sources

#### 1. Box Office Revenue
- **Data Sources**: Box office reporting services
- **Tracking**: Weekly updates during theatrical run
- **Distribution**: Quarterly distributions

#### 2. Streaming Revenue
- **Platforms**: Netflix, Amazon Prime, Disney+, etc.
- **Tracking**: Monthly reports from distributors
- **Distribution**: Quarterly distributions

#### 3. Distribution Revenue
- **Sources**: International distribution, TV rights, DVD/Blu-ray
- **Tracking**: Quarterly reports
- **Distribution**: Quarterly distributions

#### 4. Ancillary Revenue
- **Sources**: Merchandise, music rights, etc.
- **Tracking**: Annual reports
- **Distribution**: Annual distributions

### Returns Calculation

#### Formula
```
Investor Return = (Investment Amount / Total Movie Budget) × Revenue Share × Total Revenue
```

#### Example
- Movie Budget: $10M
- Investor Investment: $10,000 (1 lot = 0.1%)
- Revenue Share: 30% of net revenue
- Total Revenue: $50M
- Investor Return: ($10,000 / $10,000,000) × 0.30 × $50,000,000 = $15,000
- ROI: 50%

### Distribution Schedule
- **Quarterly**: Box office and streaming revenue
- **Semi-Annual**: Distribution revenue
- **Annual**: Ancillary revenue and final distributions

## Risk Management

### Risk Categories

#### 1. Production Risks
- **Delays**: Timeline extensions, budget overruns
- **Mitigation**: Milestone-based funding, regular monitoring

#### 2. Financial Risks
- **Budget Overruns**: Cost management, contingency planning
- **Mitigation**: Escrow accounts, regular audits

#### 3. Market Risks
- **Poor Performance**: Box office underperformance
- **Mitigation**: Diversified portfolio, realistic projections

#### 4. Legal Risks
- **Rights Issues**: Chain of title problems
- **Mitigation**: Legal due diligence, insurance

### Risk Monitoring
- Monthly risk assessments
- Quarterly risk reviews
- Annual portfolio analysis

## Escalation Procedures

### Level 1: Minor Issues
- **Handled By**: Operations team
- **Response Time**: 48 hours
- **Examples**: Content updates, minor delays

### Level 2: Moderate Issues
- **Handled By**: Operations manager
- **Response Time**: 24 hours
- **Examples**: Budget variances, timeline delays

### Level 3: Critical Issues
- **Handled By**: CEO/COO
- **Response Time**: 4 hours
- **Examples**: Major budget overruns, legal issues, production halts
