# Partnership Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines MovieFund's partnership strategy, including target partners, partnership models, and relationship management.

---

## Partnership Categories

### 1. Production Company Partnerships

#### Target Partners
- Independent production companies
- Studio-backed production houses
- International production companies
- Emerging filmmakers

#### Partnership Model
- **Exclusive Partnerships**: Preferred listing for select partners
- **Non-Exclusive**: Open platform for all qualified companies
- **Revenue Share**: Platform fee from capital raised

#### Value Proposition for Partners
- Access to new funding source
- Broader investor base
- Marketing and promotion
- Transparent platform
- Faster funding process

#### Requirements
- Registered production company
- Track record of productions
- Qualified movie projects
- Transparent financials
- Legal compliance

---

### 2. Film Festival Partnerships

#### Target Partners
- Major film festivals (Cannes, Sundance, TIFF, etc.)
- Regional film festivals

- **Booth Presence**: Exhibition space

#### Benefits
