# Movie Due Diligence Checklist

## Overview

This comprehensive checklist ensures thorough evaluation of every movie before approval for listing on the MovieFund platform.

**Review Period**: 2-3 weeks
**Review Team**: Investment Committee (CEO, COO, Legal, Finance)

## Appendix: Risk Scoring Guide

### 5-Star Rating System

**⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Excellent**
- Exceeds expectations
- Very low risk
- Strong track record
- Highly recommended

**⭐⭐⭐⭐ Good**
- Meets expectations
- Low to medium risk
- Solid track record
- Recommended

**⭐⭐⭐ Average**
- Meets basic requirements
- Medium risk
- Adequate track record
- Acceptable with conditions

**⭐⭐ Below Average**
- Below expectations
- Medium to high risk
- Weak track record
- Requires significant mitigation

**⭐ Poor**
- Does not meet requirements
- High risk
- Poor/no track record
- Not recommended
