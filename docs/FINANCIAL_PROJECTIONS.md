# Financial Projections - MovieFund

## Executive Summary

This document outlines financial projections for MovieFund over a 3-year period, including revenue, expenses, and key financial metrics.

**Projection Period**: 3 years (Year 1-3)
**Base Case Scenario**: Conservative growth
**Optimistic Scenario**: Aggressive growth
**Pessimistic Scenario**: Slow growth

---

## 1. Key Assumptions

### 1.1 Market Assumptions

**Movie Market:**
- Average movie budget: $5M
- Average raise per movie: $2M (40% of budget)
- Movies per year: Scaling from 5 to 50
- Funding success rate: 80%

**Investor Market:**
- Average investment per investor: $10K
- Average investments per investor: 2-3 movies
- Investor retention: 70%
- Investor acquisition: Scaling from 50 to 500/year

---

### 1.2 Revenue Assumptions

**Structuring Fee:**
- Percentage: 5% of capital raised
- Average per movie: $100K (5% of $2M)

**Management Fee:**
- Percentage: 1.5% of AUM annually
- Calculated on total capital raised

**Transaction Fee:**
- Percentage: 0.5% per investment
- Average per transaction: $50

**Performance Fee (Optional):**
- Percentage: 15% of returns above 10% hurdle
- Applied selectively

---

### 1.3 Cost Assumptions

**Technology:**
- Platform development: $200K-$400K
- Infrastructure: $5K-$15K/month
- Tools and software: $2K-$5K/month

**Operations:**
- Team salaries: Scaling from $300K to $1.5M/year
- Office/remote: $50K-$150K/year
- Legal and compliance: $50K-$100K/year

**Marketing:**
- Digital marketing: $10K-$30K/month
- PR and events: $5K-$15K/month
- Content creation: $3K-$8K/month
