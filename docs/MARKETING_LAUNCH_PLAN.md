# Marketing Launch Plan - MovieFund

## Executive Summary

This comprehensive marketing plan outlines the strategy to launch MovieFund, acquire investors, onboard production companies, and build brand awareness in the film investment market.

**Launch Timeline**: 3-6 months
**Target**: 50-100 investors, 3-5 pilot movies, $500K-$2M in investments

## 4. Investor Acquisition Strategy

### 4.1 Target Segments

**Primary Segments:**
1. **Film Enthusiasts**
   - Age: 30-55
   - Income: $150K+
   - Interests: Movies, entertainment
   - Channels: Film festivals, industry events

2. **High-Net-Worth Individuals**
   - Age: 40-65
   - Investable assets: $500K+
   - Interests: Alternative investments
   - Channels: Wealth management, private clubs

3. **Angel Investors**
   - Age: 35-60
   - Investment experience
   - Interests: Startups, alternative assets
   - Channels: Angel networks, events

4. **Family Offices**
   - Multi-generational wealth
   - Diversified portfolios
   - Alternative investments
   - Channels: Family office networks

### 4.3 Conversion Funnel

**Awareness → Interest → Consideration → Investment**

1. **Awareness**
   - SEO, paid ads, PR, social media
   - Goal: 10,000+ monthly visitors

2. **Interest**
   - Landing pages, content, webinars
   - Goal: 1,000+ leads/month

3. **Consideration**
   - Email sequences, demos, consultations
   - Goal: 100+ qualified leads/month

4. **Investment**
   - Onboarding, KYC, investment
   - Goal: 10-20 investors/month

**Conversion Rate Targets:**
- Website → Lead: 10%
- Lead → Qualified: 10%
- Qualified → Investor: 10-20%
