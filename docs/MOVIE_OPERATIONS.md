# Movie Operations Guide

## Overview

This guide covers the complete lifecycle of managing movies on the MovieFund platform, from initial submission through funding, production, release, and returns distribution.

---

## 1. Movie Onboarding Process

### 1.1 Initial Contact & Qualification

**Step 1: Initial Outreach**
- Production company contacts MovieFund or vice versa
- Initial conversation to understand:
  - Movie budget range
  - Production timeline
  - Director/producer track record
  - Current funding status

**Step 2: Qualification Check**
- Budget: $2M - $50M (ideal range)
- Stage: Pre-production to early production
- Director: Minimum 1-2 previous releases
- Production company: Established entity

**Step 3: NDA & Initial Agreement**
- Sign mutual NDA
- Share preliminary information
- Schedule detailed discussion

**Timeline**: 1-2 weeks

---

### 1.2 Submission & Documentation

**Required Documents:**

1. **Movie Information**
   - [ ] Title and tagline
   - [ ] Genre and language
   - [ ] Director and producer names
   - [ ] Star cast list
   - [ ] Production company details
   - [ ] Synopsis (1-2 pages)

2. **Financial Documents**
   - [ ] Detailed budget breakdown
   - [ ] Funding gap (amount needed)
   - [ ] Existing investors/partners
   - [ ] Revenue projections
   - [ ] Distribution strategy

3. **Production Documents**
   - [ ] Script (if available)
   - [ ] Production timeline
   - [ ] Shooting schedule
   - [ ] Location details
   - [ ] Key crew list

4. **Legal Documents**
   - [ ] Production company registration
   - [ ] Rights documentation
   - [ ] Distribution agreements (if any)
   - [ ] Insurance policies

5. **Marketing Materials**
   - [ ] Poster/teaser images
   - [ ] Trailer (if available)
   - [ ] Director/producer bio
   - [ ] Cast photos

**Submission Process:**
1. Production company fills submission form
2. Uploads all required documents
3. Submits for review
4. Receives confirmation email

**Timeline**: 1-2 weeks for production company to gather and submit

---

### 1.3 Due Diligence

**Internal Review Process:**

**Week 1: Initial Review**
- [ ] Document completeness check
- [ ] Budget verification
- [ ] Basic background checks
- [ ] Risk assessment

**Week 2: Deep Dive**
- [ ] Financial analysis
- [ ] Market research
- [ ] Director/producer track record analysis
- [ ] Competitive analysis
- [ ] Legal review

**Week 3: Decision**
- [ ] Investment committee review
- [ ] Approval/rejection decision
- [ ] Feedback to production company

**Due Diligence Checklist**: See [DUE_DILIGENCE_CHECKLIST.md](./DUE_DILIGENCE_CHECKLIST.md)

**Timeline**: 2-3 weeks

---

### 1.4 Approval & Contracting

**If Approved:**

1. **Partnership Agreement**
   - Negotiate terms
   - Revenue sharing percentage
   - Reporting requirements
   - Timeline commitments
   - Sign partnership agreement

2. **Movie Listing Setup**
   - Create movie profile on platform
   - Upload all materials
   - Set investment parameters:
     - Total budget
     - Amount to raise
     - Lot price (0.1% of budget)
     - Minimum/maximum investment
   - Set funding deadline

3. **Launch Preparation**
   - Marketing materials ready
   - Press release prepared
   - Social media assets
   - Email campaign ready

**Timeline**: 1-2 weeks

---

## 2. Movie Listing & Funding

### 2.1 Platform Listing

**Movie Profile Includes:**
- Title, tagline, synopsis
- Director, producer, cast
- Genre, language
- Budget breakdown
- Investment opportunity details
- Timeline and milestones
- Expected returns
- Risk factors

**Investment Parameters:**
- Total lots: 1000 (fixed)
- Price per lot: 0.1% of total budget
- Minimum investment: 1 lot
- Maximum investment: 100 lots per investor
- Funding target: Amount needed
- Funding deadline: 30-60 days

### 2.2 Funding Period

**Active Monitoring:**
- [ ] Track daily investment progress
- [ ] Monitor investor questions
- [ ] Update movie status
- [ ] Send weekly updates to production company

**Marketing Support:**
- [ ] Social media promotion
- [ ] Email campaigns
- [ ] PR outreach
- [ ] Investor webinars (optional)

**Timeline**: 30-60 days

---

### 2.3 Funding Completion

**If Fully Funded:**

1. **Fund Transfer**
   - Verify all investments received
   - Transfer funds to production company
   - Confirm receipt
   - Update movie status to "Funded"

2. **Investor Communication**
   - Send confirmation to all investors
   - Issue investment certificates
   - Provide portfolio access
   - Set up investor updates

3. **Production Company**
   - Confirm fund receipt
   - Begin production
   - Set up reporting schedule

**If Not Fully Funded:**

1. **Options:**
   - Extend funding period (if close)
   - Partial funding (if acceptable)
   - Return investments (if minimum not met)

2. **Investor Communication**
   - Notify of status
   - Process refunds if needed
   - Thank for participation

**Timeline**: 1 week

## 5. Revenue Tracking & Returns

### 5.1 Revenue Collection

**Revenue Sources:**
1. **Box Office**
   - Theatrical gross
   - International box office
   - Tracking via distributors

2. **Streaming**
   - Netflix, Amazon Prime, etc.
   - Revenue share agreements
   - Monthly reports

3. **Distribution**
   - TV rights
   - DVD/Blu-ray
   - International sales

4. **Ancillary**
   - Merchandise
   - Music rights
   - Other revenue streams

**Collection Process:**
- [ ] Monthly revenue reports from production company
- [ ] Verify against distributor reports
- [ ] Calculate platform share
- [ ] Calculate investor returns

**Timeline**: Monthly for 12-24 months post-release

---

### 5.2 Returns Calculation

**Calculation Formula:**
```
Total Revenue = Box Office + Streaming + Distribution + Ancillary
Platform Fee = Total Revenue × Platform Fee %
Net Revenue = Total Revenue - Platform Fee
Investor Share = Net Revenue × (Investor Investment / Total Budget)
Per Investor Return = Investor Share × (Investor Lots / Total Lots)
```

**Distribution:**
- [ ] Calculate returns quarterly
- [ ] Distribute to investors
- [ ] Issue tax documents
- [ ] Update investor portfolios

**Timeline**: Quarterly distributions

## 6. Movie Lifecycle Status

### Status Definitions

1. **Submitted** - Application received, under review
2. **Under Review** - Due diligence in progress
3. **Approved** - Approved, contract negotiation
4. **Listed** - Live on platform, accepting investments
5. **Funding** - Active funding period
6. **Funded** - Fully funded, production begins
7. **In Production** - Currently filming
8. **Post-Production** - Editing and post-production
9. **Pre-Release** - Marketing and distribution setup
10. **Released** - In theaters/streaming
11. **Returns Active** - Generating and distributing returns
12. **Completed** - All returns distributed, project closed

## 8. Risk Management

### Production Risks
- **Budget Overruns**
  - Mitigation: Regular monitoring, approval processes
  - Action: Early identification, budget adjustments

- **Timeline Delays**
  - Mitigation: Buffer time, milestone tracking
  - Action: Communication, timeline adjustments

- **Production Issues**
  - Mitigation: Insurance, experienced crew
  - Action: Problem-solving, alternative plans

### Financial Risks
- **Low Box Office**
  - Mitigation: Market research, realistic projections
  - Action: Diversified revenue streams

- **Distribution Issues**
  - Mitigation: Pre-negotiated deals
  - Action: Alternative distribution channels
