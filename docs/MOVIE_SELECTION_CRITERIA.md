# Movie Selection Criteria - MovieFund

## Overview

This document outlines the criteria and framework used to evaluate and select movies for listing on the MovieFund platform.

**Purpose**: Ensure quality, viability, and alignment with platform goals
**Review Process**: Investment Committee review
**Timeline**: 2-3 weeks per movie

---

## 1. Budget Criteria

### 1.1 Budget Range
- **Preferred Range**: $2M - $50M
- **Minimum**: $1M (exceptions considered)
- **Maximum**: $100M (exceptions considered)

**Rationale:**
- Too small (<$1M): Limited market potential, higher risk
- Too large (>$100M): Requires significant capital, longer funding period
- Sweet spot: $2M-$50M offers good balance of risk and return

### 1.2 Budget Breakdown
- [ ] Detailed budget provided
- [ ] Budget categories clear:
  - Pre-production: 10-15%
  - Production: 50-60%
  - Post-production: 15-20%
  - Marketing: 10-15%
  - Contingency: 10-15%
- [ ] Budget realism verified
- [ ] Comparison with similar movies

**Red Flags:**
- Unrealistic budget
- Missing categories
- No contingency
- Budget too low for scope

## 9. Overall Scoring System

### 9.1 Scoring Categories

**Each category scored 1-5:**

1. **Budget & Financials** (Weight: 20%)
   - Budget realism
   - Financial viability
   - Revenue projections
   - ROI potential

2. **Creative Team** (Weight: 25%)
   - Director track record
   - Producer track record
   - Cast marketability
   - Team quality

3. **Market Potential** (Weight: 20%)
   - Genre appeal
   - Market size
   - Competition
   - Distribution

4. **Production Viability** (Weight: 15%)
   - Timeline realism
   - Production plan
   - Risk factors
   - Execution capability

5. **Legal & Rights** (Weight: 10%)
   - Rights clearance
   - Legal compliance
   - Contracts
   - Insurance

6. **Risk Level** (Weight: 10%)
   - Overall risk assessment
   - Mitigation plans
   - Acceptability

---

### 9.2 Scoring Scale

**⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Excellent (5 points)**
- Exceeds expectations
- Very low risk
- Highly recommended

**⭐⭐⭐⭐ Good (4 points)**
- Meets expectations
- Low to medium risk
- Recommended

**⭐⭐⭐ Average (3 points)**
- Meets basic requirements
- Medium risk
- Acceptable with conditions

**⭐⭐ Below Average (2 points)**
- Below expectations
- Medium to high risk
- Requires significant mitigation

**⭐ Poor (1 point)**
- Does not meet requirements
- High risk
- Not recommended

---

### 9.3 Overall Score Calculation

**Weighted Average:**
```
Overall Score = (Budget × 0.20) + (Creative × 0.25) + (Market × 0.20) + 
                (Production × 0.15) + (Legal × 0.10) + (Risk × 0.10)
```

**Maximum Score: 5.0**

---

### 9.4 Decision Criteria

**Approve:**
- Overall score ≥ 4.0
- No critical red flags
- All key criteria met
- Risk level acceptable

**Conditional Approval:**
- Overall score 3.5-3.9
- Minor issues to resolve
- Additional information needed
- Conditions to meet

**Reject:**
- Overall score < 3.5
- Critical red flags
- Key criteria not met
- Risk level too high

## 10. Special Considerations
