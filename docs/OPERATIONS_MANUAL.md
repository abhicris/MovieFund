## 2. Investor Onboarding

### 2.1 Registration Process

**Step 1: Registration**
1. Investor visits website
2. Clicks "Invest Now" or "Sign Up"
3. Fills registration form:
   - Name, email, phone
   - Password
   - Country
   - Investment interest
4. Email verification sent
5. Investor verifies email

**Step 2: Profile Setup**
1. Investor logs in
2. Completes profile:
   - Personal information
   - Investment preferences
   - Risk tolerance
   - Source of funds
3. Saves profile

**Step 3: KYC/AML**
1. Investor initiates KYC
2. Uploads required documents:
   - Government ID
   - Proof of address
   - Source of funds (if required)
3. System processes documents
4. Manual review if needed
5. Approval/rejection notification

**Timeline**: 1-3 business days

---

### 2.2 KYC/AML Process

**Required Documents:**
- Government-issued ID (passport, driver's license)
- Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
- Source of funds (if required)
- Additional documents for high-value investments

**Verification Steps:**
1. Document upload
2. Automated verification (AI/OCR)
3. Manual review (if needed)
4. Identity verification
5. Sanctions screening
6. Approval/rejection

**Approval Criteria:**
- Valid documents
- Identity verified
- No sanctions matches
- Source of funds acceptable
- Risk assessment passed

**Rejection Reasons:**
- Invalid documents
- Identity not verified
- Sanctions match
- High risk
- Incomplete information

**Timeline**: 1-2 business days

---

### 2.3 First Investment

**Process:**
1. Investor browses movies
2. Selects movie to invest in
3. Reviews movie details
4. Selects number of lots
5. Reviews investment terms
6. Confirms investment
7. Payment processing
8. Investment confirmation
9. Portfolio updated

## 5. Returns Distribution

### 5.1 Revenue Collection

**Revenue Sources:**
- Box office
- Streaming
- Distribution
- Ancillary

**Collection Process:**
1. Production company reports revenue
2. Revenue verified
3. Documentation reviewed
4. Revenue recorded
5. Distribution calculated

**Timeline**: Quarterly

---

### 5.2 Returns Calculation

**Calculation Steps:**
1. Total revenue collected
2. Platform fees deducted
3. Production company share deducted
4. Net revenue calculated
5. Investor returns calculated (per lot)
6. Returns distributed

**Formula:**
```
Net Revenue = Total Revenue - Platform Fees - Production Company Share
Investor Return = (Net Revenue Ã— Investor Lots) / Total Lots
```

**Timeline**: Quarterly distribution

---
