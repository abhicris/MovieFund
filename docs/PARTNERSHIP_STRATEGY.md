# Partnership Strategy - MovieFund

## Overview

This document outlines MovieFund's partnership strategy to build relationships with production companies, distributors, investors, and other key stakeholders in the film industry ecosystem.

**Goal**: Establish 20-30 strategic partnerships in first 6 months

---

## 1. Production Company Partnerships

### 1.1 Target Partners

**Criteria:**
- Established production companies
- Budget range: $2M-$50M per movie
- Active production slate (2-5 movies/year)
- Open to alternative funding models
- Good track record

**Geographic Focus:**
- **Bollywood**: Mumbai-based production houses
- **Hollywood**: Independent and mid-budget studios
- **Spanish Cinema**: Production companies in Spain and Latin America
- **Other Markets**: UK, Canada, Australia

**Examples:**
- Mid-budget Bollywood producers
- Independent Hollywood studios
- Spanish production companies
- International co-producers

---

### 1.2 Partnership Model

**Value Proposition for Production Companies:**
- Access to new funding source
- Marketing and investor network
- Transparent platform
- Faster funding process
- Lower cost of capital (vs traditional financing)

**Partnership Structure:**
- **Exclusive**: Preferred partner status
- **Non-Exclusive**: Open marketplace
- **Strategic**: Deep integration, co-marketing

**Revenue Share:**
- Platform fee: 3-7% of capital raised
- Management fee: 1-2% AUM
- Performance fee: 10-20% of returns (optional)

---

## 11. Risk Management

### Partnership Risks

**Risk: Partner Quality**
- Mitigation: Thorough due diligence
- Action: Clear quality standards

**Risk: Exclusivity Conflicts**
- Mitigation: Clear terms
- Action: Flexible structures

**Risk: Low Activity**
- Mitigation: Minimum commitments
- Action: Regular engagement

**Risk: Competition**
- Mitigation: Strong value proposition
- Action: Continuous improvement
