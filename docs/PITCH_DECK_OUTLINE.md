## Slide-by-Slide Outline

### 1. Title Slide
- Company: MovieFund
- Tagline: "Democratizing Film Investment"
- Date, Presenter, Contact

### 2. The Problem
- Film investment inaccessible
- High barriers to entry
- Production companies struggle to raise capital
- Lack of transparency

### 3. The Solution
- Fractional film investment platform
- Low minimum investment
- Transparent process
- Diversified opportunities

### 4. Market Opportunity
- $100B+ global film industry
- $50B+ streaming market
- $13T+ alternative investment market
- $500M-$1B addressable market

### 5. Business Model
- Structuring fee: 3-7% of capital raised
- Management fee: 1-2% AUM
- Transaction fee: 0.5-1%
- Performance fee: 10-20% (optional)

- Platform screenshots
- Key features
- User experience

### Problem
- "Film investment is only for studios and ultra-high-net-worth individuals"
- "Average investors have no access"
- "Production companies struggle to raise capital quickly"

### Solution

- "Transparent platform connecting investors with opportunities"
