**2.1 Investor Rights**
- Right to receive returns based on revenue share

- Right to view investment portfolio

- No creative control

**2.2 Investor Obligations**
- Payment of investment amount
- Compliance with platform terms
- Accurate information provision
- KYC/AML compliance

**2.3 Production Company Obligations**
- Use funds solely for movie production
- Provide regular progress updates
- Report revenue accurately
- Distribute returns as agreed
- Maintain proper records

**2.4 MovieFund Obligations**
- Facilitate investment process
- Provide platform services
- Distribute returns to investors
- Maintain investor records
- Provide customer support
